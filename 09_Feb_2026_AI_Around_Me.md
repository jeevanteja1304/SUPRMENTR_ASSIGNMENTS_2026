Real-Life AI Applications

1. Google Maps Navigation
Uses machine learning to analyze traffic patterns and predict the fastest route.

2. YouTube Recommendations
ML models analyze watch history and user behavior to recommend videos.

3. Netflix Suggestions
Uses collaborative filtering to recommend movies based on user preferences.

4. Voice Assistants (Alexa / Google Assistant)
Natural Language Processing helps understand voice commands.

5. Email Spam Filter
Machine learning classifies emails as spam or normal based on patterns.

6. Face Unlock on Smartphones
Computer vision models detect and match facial features.

7. Instagram Feed Ranking
AI ranks posts based on engagement and interaction patterns.

8. Amazon Product Recommendations
ML suggests products based on browsing and purchase history.

9. Autocorrect on Smartphones
NLP models predict the most likely word based on context.

10. Online Ads Personalization
AI predicts which ads a user is most likely to click.
